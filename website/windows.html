FAQ

WINDOW INSTALLATION FAQS

Get answers to the most common questions about window replacement and installation. Still have questions? Contact us for a free consultation.

Signs you need new windows include: drafts or cold spots near windows, difficulty opening or closing, visible condensation between panes, increased energy bills, or windows that are 15-20+ years old. We offer free inspections to assess your windows' condition.

We install all window types including double-hung, casement, sliding, picture, bay, bow, awning, and specialty shapes. We work with leading manufacturers to offer vinyl, fiberglass, wood, and aluminum-clad options.

Most window replacements take just one day. A typical home with 10-15 windows can be completed in 6-8 hours. Larger projects or custom installations may take 2-3 days. We'll provide a specific timeline during your estimate.

Low-E (low emissivity) glass has a microscopic coating that reflects heat while allowing light through. It keeps your home cooler in summer and warmer in winter. In Utah and Arizona's climate, Low-E glass is highly recommended for energy savings.

Homeowners typically see 25-30% reduction in heating and cooling costs after installing energy-efficient windows. The exact savings depend on your current windows, home size, and local climate conditions.

Yes! We offer flexible financing options to make your window investment affordable. We partner with several financing companies to provide competitive rates and terms that fit your budget.

Our windows come with comprehensive manufacturer warranties, typically lifetime on the frame and 20+ years on the glass. We also provide our own workmanship warranty covering installation quality.

Yes! Quality double or triple-pane windows significantly reduce outside noise. This is especially noticeable for homes near busy streets, airports, or commercial areas. Many customers are surprised by how much quieter their home becomes.

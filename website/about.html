Our Story

SERVICES BUILT TO LAST

Relentless Construction started with a simple belief: homeowners deserve contractors who stand by their word, do what they promise, and stand behind their work. Period.

What began as a small roofing operation has grown into a full-service construction company serving homeowners across Utah and Arizona. We've never lost the challenger spirit or work ethic that got us here.

We're not the cheapest option out there — and we never will be. What we are is reliable, skilled, and absolutely relentless when it comes to quality. That's why 3 out of 4 homeowners have trusted us with their biggest investments, and why we maintain a 5-Star rating across thousands of reviews.

SCOTTSDALE ROOFING SERVICES

Concrete & Clay Tile

The gold standard for Arizona. 50+ year service life. We re-felt with high-temp underlayment when existing tiles can be re-laid — saving thousands.

Foam (SPF) Roofing

Spray polyurethane foam for flat and low-slope desert homes. Seamless, reflective, recoatable every 10-15 years instead of full replacement.

Cool-Roof Shingles

Class 4 impact-rated, cool-roof rated asphalt — built to handle monsoon hail and reflect desert heat.

Monsoon Storm Damage

Same-day emergency tarping during monsoon season. Full insurance documentation and claim advocacy.

Repairs & Re-felt

Targeted repairs and tile re-felts that extend roof life by 20+ years at a fraction of replacement cost.
